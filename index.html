POSE ESTIMATION

Online REBA score estimation system

Technology : Python, Flask, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, OpenCV, SQL

Problem Statement : REBA stands for Rapid Entire Body Assessment. It is a tool used to evaluate the risk of musculoskeletal disorders (MSDs) associated with specific tasks within a job. Currently, companies hire ergonomics to perform this assessment on employees at regular intervals of time. The manual and non-real-time evaluation are ineffective methods. It requires a solution that can dynamically assess the human posture at work and report in case of bad posture, so that company can enact proactively.

Solution : Created a web based application where the companies can upload their video clips and can get real-time REBA score. The solution also include frame by frame breakdown of the video supported by interactive visualisation. This provide the company a holistic view and actionable KPIs to ensure the safety of their employees.

Technical Aspect : The front-end include 5 views including registration, login, upload page, video manager and REBA Report. It extensively uses HTML, CSS, Java-Script, and D3js. The app uses python based Flask framework in back-end to manage the API calls. For data management we used a relational database to store all the user related data.                           

For pose estimation, we used Google's machine learning framework Media Pipe. The inference data is consumed by a custom algorithm to calculate angles, adding color indicators and estimating score for each frame.

SURVEY AnaLYSER

Problem Statement

Imagine your business to have got 100+ customer reviews. They would have different
words & even varying tones of customers — from good to bad or even worse. This analysis could be done manually, but, as the volume of unstructured text increases, the manual approach to text analytics becomes ineffective and unproductive. The amount
of labour required to carry out text analytics makes the manual approach inefficient.

Highlights

Theme Identifier Identifies the key phrases/themes present in your text, enabling team to get overview of what is present in the data without any human bias. Such an overarching approach to identifying topics of interest would enable a business to identify positive and negative topics of high impact that can be improved immediately

Sentiments Analyser : Analyzes text and reports on the presence of negative, positive, and neutral sentiment, so organizations know how people feel about their offerings

Customisation : Allows you to fully customize the themes/sentiments using merge/edit feature based on your business understanding Tagger: Let’s you create tags so that important business terms can be analyzed properly

Visualisation : Dashboard to show you top themes, sentiments & their distribution across other dimensions

Project Panel : shows all your projects in single place Combine Projects : Allows to merge multiple projects to analyze the theme evolution over data collected in multiple periods of time
